## Logo Prompt (Primary Mark)

> Design a minimal, modern personal brand logo for **"marcothesis"** — the portfolio identity of a marketing strategist specializing in data, consumer intelligence, and AI across Asia-Pacific.
>
> **Wordmark approach:** The name "marcothesis" as a clean wordmark in lowercase. The typeface should feel like **Inter** — geometric sans-serif, medium weight, generous letter-spacing. Consider a subtle typographic detail: the "t" could have a slightly extended crossbar, or the dot on the "i" could be replaced with a small geometric accent — but keep it restrained, not playful.
>
> **Accent color:** Terracotta/burnt orange `#D4703A` as the sole brand color. Use it sparingly — perhaps only on one letter, a geometric detail, or a separating element. The rest of the wordmark should be near-black `#1C1710`.
>
> **Optional brandmark:** A standalone geometric icon that works as a monogram or symbol — could be an abstract "M" or "mt" ligature, or a minimal shape that suggests *bridging a gap* (the brand's through-line: turning ambiguity into working solutions). The shape should work at 16x16px for favicon use.
>
> **Background:** Warm cream `#FAF6F0` (light mode) or deep warm brown `#17130E` (dark mode). Show both.
>
> **Style:** Warm, confident, understated. Think senior strategist — not agency-flashy, not startup-playful. Closer to a consulting firm's restraint than a creative studio's exuberance. No gradients, no 3D effects, no illustrations. Flat, typographic, geometric.
>
> **Deliverables:** Wordmark (horizontal), standalone brandmark/icon, both on light and dark backgrounds.

## Favicon Prompt (16x16 / 32x32 / 192x192)

> Design a favicon for **"marcothesis"** that works at very small sizes (16x16px).
>
> Use the standalone brandmark from the logo — either an abstract "M" monogram or the geometric accent element. Must be instantly recognizable in a browser tab.
>
> **Colors:** Terracotta `#D4703A` mark on cream `#FAF6F0` background (light), or on dark brown `#17130E` (dark). Also provide a version with transparent background.
>
> **Constraint:** Must remain legible and distinctive at 16x16 pixels. Avoid fine lines or small details that disappear at that scale.
